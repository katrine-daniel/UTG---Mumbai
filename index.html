Frequently Asked Questions

The distance between Delhi and Mumbai by car is approximately 1,450 kilometers via NH48. The journey typically takes 24-30 hours depending on traffic conditions, route taken, and stops made along the way.

Multiple daily flights operate between Delhi and Mumbai. Major airlines include IndiGo, Air India, Vistara, and SpiceJet. Flight duration is approximately 2 hours.

Popular food stops include:

  • Jaipur - Try dal baati churma
  • Udaipur - Local thalis
  • Vadodara - Gujarati snacks
  • Surat - Surti locho and sev puri

The cheapest option is sleeper class train, with ticket prices starting around ₹600–₹800. Book in advance for better deals.
